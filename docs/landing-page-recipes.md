Choose the closest finished page first, then compose the new route from canonical components. Section content can change; section markup and visual behavior should not.

## Industry page

Reference `/industry/fashion`. Reuse its editorial hero, statistics, logo proof, narrative chapters, testimonial, capabilities, CTA, and FAQ structures. Promote a new industry pattern to a reusable component before using it twice.

## Comparison page

Reference the finished `/vs/*` routes. Reuse their comparison hero, verdict, tables, decision guide, migration section, FAQ, and CTA. Keep competitor claims sourced and current.

## Copy rules

- Lead with the operating outcome.
- Name Shopify objects precisely: products, variants, collections, metafields, metaobjects, media, and stores.
- Keep headings short and body copy concrete.
- Use one primary CTA label per page, normally “Get Peak PIM” or “Try for free.”
- Do not invent customer quotes, quantified outcomes, pricing, limits, or integrations.

`/partners` is unfinished and must never be used as a source or approved reference.
