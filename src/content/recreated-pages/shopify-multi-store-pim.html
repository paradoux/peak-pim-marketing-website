FAQ

Everything you need to know about managing multiple Shopify stores with Peak PIM.

How many Shopify stores can I connect?

It depends on your plan. Core supports 2 stores, Elite supports 5, Scale supports 8, and Enterprise supports unlimited stores. You can connect additional stores at any time from your Peak PIM dashboard.

How does the 1-click setup work for multiple stores?

Install Peak PIM on each of your Shopify stores. They connect automatically and your products are linked across storefronts. No manual mapping, no CSV imports, no technical setup required.

How are products matched across stores?

Peak PIM links products across your stores automatically. Once connected, your catalog is unified and ready to manage from one place.

Can I control which products sync to which stores?

Yes. You can choose which products are shared across all stores and which ones stay specific to a single storefront. Full control over what gets pushed where.

What happens if I have different prices per store?

Peak PIM respects per-store pricing. You can update product data like titles, descriptions, and metafields across stores while keeping prices independent per storefront.

Can I bulk edit products across all my stores at once?

Yes. Peak PIM's bulk editor lets you select products across all connected stores and edit them in one action: titles, descriptions, variants, metafields and more.

Does Peak PIM work with Shopify Plus expansion stores?

Yes. Peak PIM works with all Shopify plans including Shopify Plus expansion stores. Connect all your storefronts regardless of how they're structured.

What happens to my existing product data when I connect a new store?

Peak PIM pulls in your existing catalog automatically when you connect a store. Your data is ready to manage from day one. No manual import required.

Does metafield sync work across all stores?

Yes. You can build your metafield schema once and apply it across every connected store. Edit metafield values in bulk across all storefronts simultaneously.

Is multi-store management included in all plans?

Yes. Every Peak PIM plan supports multiple stores. The number of stores you can connect scales with your plan. From 2 stores on Core up to unlimited on Enterprise.
