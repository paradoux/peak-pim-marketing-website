Start fresh or plug into an existing PIM.

Once Peak PIM is connected to your Shopify store, you choose how to get started. Create a brand new PIM populated with your catalog automatically, or connect to an existing PIM (made with Peak PIM via another store).

New PIM

A new PIM is created and filled with your entire Shopify catalog. Ready to use in seconds.

Existing PIM

Connect to a PIM you already have created. Products are matched automatically by name or SKU, and anything unmatched is created as a new entry.

Bring your own data. Peak PIM handles the rest.

Upload a CSV or XLSX file and Peak PIM maps it to your catalog automatically. Existing products are updated, new ones are created. No manual matching required.

Updates

Existing products, variants, and collections are refreshed with the data from your file.

Creates

Products that don't exist yet are added automatically as new entries in your PIM.

FAQ

Everything you need to know about 1-click setup.

What happens to unmatched products?

If no match is found by name or SKU, the product is created as a new entry in your PIM. Nothing is lost.

Which file formats are supported?

CSV and XLSX. Both formats work the same way, use whichever your team already has.

What if some data is missing?

Peak PIM imports exactly what exists in your Shopify store. Empty fields stay empty and can be filled in later. Nothing is assumed.

Can I undo the import process?

Yes. Contact our support team and we'll help you review or revert any import.

Does 1-click setup work with custom fields?

Yes. Metafields and custom attributes are pulled in automatically alongside your standard product data.
