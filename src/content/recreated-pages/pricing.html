Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about Peak PIM and getting started.

Is there a free trial?

Yes. Every plan comes with a 10-day free trial. No credit card required to get started.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect immediately and billing is adjusted accordingly.

How many stores can I connect?

Core supports 2 stores, Elite supports 5, and Scale supports 8. Need more? Contact us about an Enterprise plan.

Is there an annual billing option?

Yes. Paying annually saves you 2 months compared to monthly billing. Core is $990/year, Elite $2,490/year, and Scale $4,990/year.

What happens if I exceed my SKU limit?

We'll let you know before you hit the limit. You can upgrade your plan at any time to unlock higher SKU limits without losing any of your data.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 10-day trial so you can test the product before committing. If you have an issue after subscribing, reach out and we'll sort it out.

Does it work with all Shopify plans?

Yes. Peak PIM works with any Shopify plan, from Basic to Plus. Some features like metaobjects require Shopify to support them on your plan.

What is included in Enterprise?

Enterprise plans include custom SKU limits, unlimited stores, dedicated support, and onboarding. Pricing is tailored to your business. Contact us to discuss.

Will pricing change over time?

Your current plan price is locked in. If we update pricing in the future, we'll give existing customers plenty of notice before any changes apply.
