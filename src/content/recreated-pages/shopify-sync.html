FAQ

Everything you need to know about Shopify sync in Peak PIM.

Does Peak PIM sync metafields and metaobjects?

Yes. Metafields, metaobject definitions, and their values are all pulled in during import. No manual mapping required.

Will importing my Shopify data overwrite anything in Peak PIM?

No. Importing pulls your Shopify data into Peak PIM. Nothing is pushed back to your store until you explicitly hit publish.

What happens if my team makes changes directly in Shopify admin?

Those changes won't automatically appear in Peak PIM. Use the manual refresh to pull them in or schedule regular refreshes to stay current.

Can I connect multiple Shopify stores?

Yes. Peak PIM supports multiple stores. Each one connects independently and you manage them all from a single workspace.

Which Shopify data types does Peak PIM sync?

Products, variants, collections, metafields, metaobjects, files, media, tags, and SEO fields. If it lives in your Shopify store, Peak PIM can sync it.

Can I create new products in Peak PIM and push them to Shopify?

Yes. Create new products, collections, and variants directly in Peak PIM and publish them to Shopify with one click.

How is Peak PIM different from a Shopify connector?

Most PIMs connect to Shopify through a third-party connector that requires API setup and misses data types. Peak PIM is a native Shopify app, it installs from the App Store and connects in seconds.
