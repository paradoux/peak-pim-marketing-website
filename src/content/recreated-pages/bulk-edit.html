Questions

Everything you need to know about bulk editing in Peak PIM.

What can I bulk edit?

Pretty much everything. Products, variants, prices, collections, images, files, SEO metadata, translations and more. All editable at once across your entire Shopify catalog. Filter by type, tag, vendor, status, or metafields to target exactly what you need, then apply your changes in one go.

Does bulk edit work across stores?

Yes. If you manage multiple Shopify stores, Peak PIM syncs changes across all of them simultaneously. One edit, every store updated.

Can I edit metafields in bulk?

Yes. Update metafields and metaobjects across your entire catalog without leaving Peak PIM. Changes sync to Shopify instantly.

Can I bulk edit variant metafields?

Yes. Peak PIM supports bulk editing of variant metafields across your entire catalog. This is one of the most common gaps in Shopify's native editor, which only handles product-level metafields in bulk.

Can I bulk update SEO meta titles and descriptions?

Yes. Peak PIM lets you bulk edit SEO metadata across all your products and collections at once. Not just products, collections too, which most tools overlook entirely.

Is there a limit on how many products I can edit at once?

No hard limit. Whether you have 100 or 10,000 products, Peak PIM handles the whole catalog.

Will changes go live immediately or can I preview first?

You can preview all changes before anything is published. Save your edits in Peak PIM first, review them, and publish to Shopify when you're ready. No surprises, no live mistakes.

Can I schedule bulk edits?

Bulk edits apply immediately. Scheduled edits are on our roadmap. If it's a priority for you, let us know and we'll bump it up.
