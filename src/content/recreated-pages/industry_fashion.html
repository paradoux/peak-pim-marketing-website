Questions

Common questions from Shopify fashion brands.

Can I manage size and color variants in bulk?

Yes. Select a full collection and update size names, color options, prices, or metafields across every variant in one action. This is the core of what Peak PIM does for Shopify fashion catalogs.

Can I store a different size chart for each region?

Yes. Store per-market size charts and fit notes as Shopify metafields, then bulk assign them to the right products and variants. Shoppers see the correct sizing for their location.

Can I organize products by season, collection, or drop?

Yes. Group products by season, collection, or drop, then assign hundreds of SKUs to Shopify collections at once instead of one at a time.

Can I bulk edit variant metafields?

Yes. Peak PIM supports bulk editing of variant metafields across your whole catalog. Shopify's native bulk editor focuses on product-level metafields, so variant-level edits are where a PIM adds the most value.

Can I manage lookbook and product images at scale?

Yes. Upload a full shoot, then assign images to products and variants in bulk by colorway and shot type. Your Shopify product media stays organized across the whole catalog.

Does it work across multiple Shopify stores?

Yes. If you run regional or multi-brand Shopify stores, Peak PIM keeps product data consistent across all of them. One edit updates every connected store.

How many products can I edit at once?

There is no hard limit. Whether your catalog has 100 or 10,000 products, Peak PIM handles the whole thing. Filter by type, tag, collection, or metafield to target exactly what you need.

Can I preview changes before they go live on Shopify?

Yes. Make your edits in Peak PIM, review them, and publish to Shopify when you are ready. Nothing goes live until you push it.
